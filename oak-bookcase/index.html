Oak Bookcase

An Arts and Crafts style fumed oak collapsible bookcase

[gallery ids="71,70,72" type="rectangular" link="none"]

This bookcase can be easily disassembled and assembled without tools for transport or storage.

The oak has been fumed with ammonia, by placing it in a plastic tent with a dish of household ammonia.
