Vineyard Table

A folding trestle table, ideal for alfresco eating

This practical and elegant table is based on a traditional design, apparently used in french vineyards for the pickers during harvest time. Usually big enough to seat six, this version was scaled down to seat four comfortably and be easily carried through a standard doorway so you can easily use it inside or out.

Built in European Oak with Sapele accents, with a hardwax oil finish.
