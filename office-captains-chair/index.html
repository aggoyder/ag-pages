Office Captain's Chair

Whilst the modern office chair is the obvious ergonomic choice to put in front of your desk at home, they seldom blend with your home decor. Meanwhile the alternative of a traditional captains chair offers  some wood and leather but look like they belong in a Victorian gentleman's club rather than a contemporary study or sitting room.

I developed this chair for my own sitting room, retaining the base of the previous functional office model and adding  a new seat. The swept back arms allows it to tuck into the desk when typing, whilst the generous seat, arms and supportive back rest make it comfortable when sitting back to watch videos. It also complements the rest of the decor!
