'River' Coffee Table

Every one is unique, dictated by the natural 'waney' edge of the board that forms the shores, inlets and topology of the river. Tinted safety glass let into the top follows the same sinuous path and allows you to see all the detail of the tree whilst conjuring the sense water cutting through a landscape.  Wood and glass conjuring those basic elements of earth and water

Natural wood slab furniture is very popular, however waney edged tables are not often practical, with their rough edges snagging clothes as you pass. The river table approach allows a balance of featuring the natural wood whilst providing a practical usable table, and can be applied to any size and type of table.
