Sycamore Sheraton

Contemporary pale wood twist to a traditional design

By taking the classic clean lines of a Sheraton carver and building it in a pale wood I hope I have produced a perfect bedroom or dressing table chair. The inlayed pinstripes in the back splats and leg details complete its refined looks.

A fully upholstered seat with coil springs and traditional materials, ensures that a comfortable seat which will last a life time.
